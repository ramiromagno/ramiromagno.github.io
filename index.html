About me

Hi I’m Ramiro, it is great to have you here.

I am a computational biologist working on the simulation of morphogenetic systems and on the analysis of Omics data of cancer samples. Currently I divide my time between wrapping-up my thesis and breast cancer data analysis at the Cancer Functional Genomics Laboratory, CBMR, under the supervision of Ana Teresa Maia.

Drop me an email at ramiro.magno@gmail.com.
